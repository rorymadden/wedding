Venue

Waterford Castle

Castle or Lodge

There are two options for accommodation for the weekend. For those only staying just the saturday night the castle has a number of very luxurious rooms. For those staying two nights it is better to stay in the lodges.

The Castle

The ceremony and reception will be taking place in the castle. Breakfast is included with each room. There are a number of different room types available:

  • Suite - €210
  • Superior - €200
  • Deluxe - €180
  • Standard - €165

The Lodges

The lodges are approximately 10 minutes walk from the castle. Each lodge contains three bedrooms.

The lodges cost €120 - €175 per room for two nights, depending on whether there are two or three rooms occupied.
